How to make Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage and lean ground beef
  • 1 Onion and 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 Tbsps Sugar
  • parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg

Step by Step instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
